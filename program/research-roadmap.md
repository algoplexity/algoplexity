## Findings Per Horizon

### Foundation: The Existence Proof (2023)
- **Discovery:** Financial markets contain discoverable algorithmic structures (Rule 131, Pair 35/115) invisible to standard econometrics.
- **Method:** Genetic Algorithms (Search) + MILS Encoding.
- **Artifact:** [Discovering Hidden Structures in Stock Market Data](https://doi.org/10.13140/RG.2.2.22740.46722).

### Horizon 0: The Somatic Marker (2025a)
- **Discovery:** "Less is More." High-resolution multivariate models (VAR) fail due to parameter explosion; a simple univariate predictive error proxy works better.
- **Physics:** Identified the "Pain" signal ($dP/dt$) — the point where the environment outpaces the model ($\Lambda > \eta_K$).
- **Artifact:** [SSRN Working Paper](https://ssrn.com/abstract=5914102).

### Horizon 1: The Cognitive EEG (2025b)
- **Discovery:** Taxonomy of Cognitive Failure — Cognitive Saturation (Rule 54 / Colliding Solitons) and Cognitive Overload (Rule 60 / Fractal Shattering).
- **NL Perspective:** Engineered the Slow Context Flow; achieved **−29.95%** early-warning lead time on unseen out-of-sample data.
- **Artifact:** [Computational Phase Transitions](https://github.com/algoplexity/computational-phase-transitions).

### Horizon 2: The Reflective Physicist (Current)
- **Discovery:** Entropic Valuation & Plasticity. An agent that adjusts its "Wingspan" (Uncertainty) based on the Physicist's diagnosis outperforms statistical baselines by **+19.0%**.
- **Physics:** Solves the Frequency Gap via Nested Optimization; three-system architecture (System 0 / System 1 / System 2).
- **Artifact:** [The QCEA-AIXI Agent](https://github.com/algoplexity/qcea-aixi-agent).

### Horizon 3: The Graph-Theoretic Future
- **Hypothesis:** Systemic Risk is a Graph Neural Cellular Automata (GNCA). A market crash is the propagation of a specific computational state (Rule 54/Default) across the asset topology.
- **Goal:** Engineer Deep GNCA Titans — graph nodes that are self-referential learners.
- **Applications:**
  - **UCL:** Modeling Algorithmic Monoculture — strategy convergence and Flash Crashes
  - **ANU:** Cybernetic Governance — systemic dashboard for institutional coherence
  - **Oxford INET:** Complexity Economics — evolving computation in supply chains
